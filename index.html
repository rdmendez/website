About Me

I am Roger Mendez and I am a student at Florida Gulf Coast University. I am currently majoring in Computer Information Systems with a programming concentration. I have aspirations to become a future programmer and have had my mind set on it ever since my middle school days. My uncle is a computer programmer and seeing what he does has pushed me to want to do the same. I find it really interesting that programming is what allows websites and applications to operate. I want to be able to create and develop such programs and apps. It would be awesome to receive recognition for a program that people put to everyday use. I would take pride in that and I know that with my work ethic and knowledge, I will be able to be recognized for it one day. I am willing to learn and put in countless hours with any project I am tasked to complete. I played four years of basketball in high school, and despite only measuring 5'6, I was a starter for the Varsity team my final two years. During my junior year, I had complications from what should've been a simple surgery for appendicitis. I was told I could not play that entire year because of an enlarged heart that was the result of the surgery.
